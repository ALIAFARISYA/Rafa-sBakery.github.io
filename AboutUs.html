Our Blogs

"At Rafa's Bakery, we believe that the best things in life shouldn't be rushed. What started as a passion for the perfect crust has grown into a local sanctuary for bread lovers. Every loaf we bake follows a slow, 48-hour fermentation process using only the finest local grains."

History

Our bakery, which is the heart and soul of baker, uses traditional techniques with modern technology on specially imported Japanese flour and natural yeast starters for all our European bread selections and Asian bun varieties.

We believe classics such as a piece of well-made baguette or croissant are the simplest yet most difficult to master. You will often find international master chefs working with our in-house team to better our skills and exchange baking knowledge.

  • 2020: Started from the kitchen of a small house with a borrowed oven.
  • 2022: Opening our first small but warm store.
  • 2026: Serve thousands of customers and continue to innovate.
